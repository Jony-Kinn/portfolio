Hello, I'm Molibeli David Kolisang

I'm a passionate rapper, music producer, and creative visionary from Maseru, Lesotho. I blend rhythm and storytelling to create tracks that speak to the soul and reflect the truths of everyday life.

Every beat I compose and every lyric I write is inspired by the values instilled in me by my late uncle—love, discipline, and unity. My journey through music is one of growth, resilience, and a relentless drive to uplift others through sound.

Beyond music, I value self-reflection, spirituality, and the endless pursuit of knowledge. Thank you for stopping by.
